ESB Events

Most famous ESB events you should'nt miss as a student at the ESB!

"Huettenzauber"- Ski- and Snowboardtrip

The Ski- and Snowboardtrip is an an amazing experience for everyone who loves skiing and/or Snowboarding. It is a weekend, organized by fellow students and it includes a hotel room, a skipass and a lot of fun activities.

Freshmen Hunt

The Student Committe of the ESB traditionally welcomes the freshmen with the "Freshmen Hunt". It is a great way to get to know each other, students from higher semester and student initiatives.

Make sure to follow the Student Committe on Instagram to don't miss on upcoming events
StuColive

Cycling for Charity

Donating for a good cause is a tradition at ESB Business School: Every year the students of ESB at Reutlingen University raise funds by organising a variety of activities for children in need. In 2022 the ESB cycling tour for charity will be travelling 1000km from Reutlingen to the North of Germany in order to raise money for two partner charities

ESB Facilities

Meet people at the campus

Canteen & cafeteria

The canteen on campus offers healthy and wholesome daily menus. Students enjoy a varied menu that includes fresh, high quality ingredients, tasty sugar free home bakes and daily snack tubs of juicy cut fruits
Students can see the weekly menu under UniNow

Campus library

The campus library is the student's gateway to learning.Over 150,000 printed books, 28,000 licensed e-journals, 100,000 e-books and numerous fulltext databases are available on campus in the University Library. The media and services of both the University Library and the Computer and Media Centre can be found in the Learning Centre
